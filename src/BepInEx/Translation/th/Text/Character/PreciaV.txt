##Profile##
【魔法】中衛で、正義の光で邪な魂を祓う勧善の戦乙女。多彩な支援効果で味方を支え、継続ダメージを与える聖浄の光が戦乙女の聖槍を喚び寄せ、悪をこらしめる。=[Magic] A righteous Valkyrie in the midline who purges wicked souls with the light of justice. Using a myriad of buffs, she supports her allies, her Sacred Purifying Light inflicts continuous damage and calls forth holy spears to punish evil.
