##Profile##
【魔法】中衛で、寄る辺無き魂を繋ぎ導く清廉の戦乙女。ダメージと共に敵の魔防を削ぎ、継続ダメージを与える聖浄の光が戦乙女の聖槍を降り注がせ、敵を清め祓う。=[Magic] A pure Valkyrie in the midline who guides and connects wandering souls. As she deals damage and strips away enemies' M.DEF, her Sacred Purifying Light inflicts continuous damage and calls down holy spears to cleanse and purge her foes.
